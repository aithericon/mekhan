Copper C110 (Electrolytic Tough Pitch) — Datasheet
===================================================

Composition : 99.90% Cu (min), 0.04% O (typical)
Density     : 8.96 g/cm^3
E-modulus   : 117 GPa
Conductivity: 101% IACS
Temper      : H02 (half hard)
Notes       : Excellent electrical + thermal conductivity; common bus-bar
              and electrical-contact stock.
