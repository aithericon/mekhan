# Contributing

## License of contributions (inbound = outbound)

By contributing to a crate in this repository, you agree that your
contribution is licensed under the **same license as that crate** (see
[`LICENSING.md`](./LICENSING.md)):

- Contributions to Apache-2.0 crates are licensed under Apache-2.0.
- Contributions to `service` are licensed under FSL-1.1-ALv2 (which carries
  the same Apache-2.0 future-license grant for the contributed code).
- Contributions to `shared/apalis` are licensed under MIT OR Apache-2.0,
  consistent with upstream.

This inbound=outbound model, combined with the Developer Certificate of Origin
below, preserves the project's ability to honor the FSL future-license grant
and to offer the proprietary enterprise/cloud components separately. Do not
contribute code you cannot certify under the DCO.

## Developer Certificate of Origin

Every commit must be signed off. Add a `Signed-off-by` trailer using your real
name and email:

    git commit -s -m "your message"

producing:

    Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>
